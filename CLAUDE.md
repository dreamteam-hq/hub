# dreamteam-hq/hub

The Nexus — central clearing house for blogs, updates, announcements, and cross-team activity across the DreamTeams pantheon.

Like the Plane of Knowledge in EverQuest or the Nexus in WoW — the place where all paths converge, everyone can find each other, and the bulletin board tells you what's happening across the world.

## What lives here

- **Blogs** — writeups, release notes, architectural deep dives
- **Updates** — cross-team announcements, status reports
- **Guides** — onboarding, how things work, where to find things

## What does NOT live here

- Agent definitions (live in agent repos: iris, sia, etc.)
- Brain schemas (live in brain-domains)
- Operational tickets (live in ops)
- Corpus content (lives in learning/learning-corpus)
